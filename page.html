Recently, Freshers Party

Aravali Institute of Technical Studies organized Fresher's Party for B. tech and Diploma first year students on Saturday, 19th November 2022. The Chief Guest for the event was Shri N. L. Khetan, Secretary, Aravali Group of Colleges. The special guests for the day were Dr. Chandrakant Sharma, Principal, Aravali college of teacher's training, Shri Shailesh Gangawat, Principal, Vedanshi college of nursing and Smt. Suman Ma'am, Vice Principal, Aravali college of teacher's training. Dr. Hemant Dhabhai, Director, AITS, addressed the audience in the beginning. Then the students of first and second year of B. Tech and Diploma participated enthusiastically in the event and gave outstanding performances. Students performed Ramp Walk, dressed in Western outfits. Based on the Ramp walk and Question-Answer round, judges chose the winners and Runner ups from a group of thirty-two students. Shivam Kumar became Mr. Fresher's (Runner Up) and Vaishnavi Pandey, Ms. Fresher's (Runner Up). Areen Chand was awarded as Mr. Fresher 2022 and Vartika Mali as Ms. Fresher. The whole event was coordinated by Mr. Gourav Purohit, ADSW.

ABOUT ARAVALI

Aravali Institute of Technical Studies, established in 2008, is an ISO9001 : 2015 certified Engineering College that is committed to provide best teaching, best training, best placements and best infrastructure for techincal education viz. B.Tech. , M.Tech., MCA & Diploma (Engineering). It is approved by AICTE, Govt. of India and Affiliated to Rajasthan Technical University, Kota (Raj). The Institution has Excellent imposing buildings with spacious Class Rooms, Highly Qualified and Experienced Faculties, Laboratories with the State of the Art equipment and other Excellent Infrastructure Facility.
